About Upsilon Pi Epsilon (UPE)

The International Honor Society for the Computing & Information Disciplines, or better known as Upsilon Pi Epsilon, was founded at Texas A&M University on January 10, 1967. Our mission is to recognize academic excellence at both the undergraduate and graduate levels in the Computing and Information Displines. Upsilon Pi Epsilon is endorsed by both the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) and the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM).
